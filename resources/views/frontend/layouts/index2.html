• Camera (Live Video & Image Capture): We use your device’s camera to allow you to record live videos directly within the app and to capture or scan images that you choose to add. Camera access is enabled only when you actively use these features.
  • Microphone (Audio Recording): The microphone is used only during video or audio recordings where voice is required. Audio is recorded only while recording is in progress and is never accessed in the background.
  • Phone Number: Your phone number is collected for account creation, identification, security, and authentication purposes. It is also used to enable contact-based features within the app.
  • Contacts: We access your phone contacts to help you identify people you know who are already using the app and to allow you to invite contacts who are not yet using the app to share your time capsule. You may select specific contacts at any time from your phone book to share your time capsule, and you can add one or multiple contacts again in the future as needed. Contact information is used solely to provide this functionality and is not permanently stored on our servers.
  • Photos, Videos, and Audio Content: We collect and store media content only when you choose to create or upload it within the app, including content you create or share through capsules.
  • User-Generated Text Content: This includes messages, notes, lists, milestones, prompts, and any other text content you create and store within the app.
  • Account Information: We collect basic account details such as email address, password, and profile information to create, manage, and secure your account.
  • Customer Support Communications: Messages you send to customer support are collected only for the purpose of responding to your requests and improving our services.
